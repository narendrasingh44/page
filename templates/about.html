Skill Development and Vocational Training Institute of India (SDVTII) is envisaged to provide skill-based and vocational courses to individual/student across India and spread its presence in world. SDVTII focuses on IT Programs like Computer Application Training, Hardware Training and many more; Non-IT Programs like Taxation, Entrepreneurship, Stock Market and many more; Sector Specialization programs like Teacher Training, Electric and Electronic Trade, Tailoring Trade, Beautician Trade and many more.

SDVTII in association with Business Training Board of nine states – Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Jammu and Kashmir, West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Goa and Karnataka; provides Certification to students for these various programs. This certification helps the students in procuring good opportunities for job and entrepreneurship.

The Main Motto of SDVTII is 'Skilled India Successful India'.

Our Vision: To UPSKILL INDIA by providing Skill Development Programs and certifications for the same. We work to capitalize India as the Power Hub Nation in regard with Skilled Human Resource.

Our Mission: UP FOR SKILLS – We are teamed to charter the needs of the society by providing a platform where individuals are trained towards skills needed in Industry. We have conceptualized the means of providing training by Offline and Online Mode. We are motivated to establish this hybrid model in our Institute and also to train and educate more than 10000 students by the end of 2017. We are also looking forward to affiliate 9 key educational institutes for Vocational Training by the end of 2017 in 9 states of India.

Our Commitment: We pledge to become a contributor in Skill India Mission so as to make India as Skill Capital of the World.
